FAQ

Starspot is a subscription service which is automatically renewed every week or day (as appropriate) unless you unsubscribe. You can terminate your subscription whenever you want only by entering in your personal menu on 100% sport (icon at top right of the display) and clicking on Leave Starspot link. Depending on your mobile operator, you may terminate your subscription by sending a SMS.
