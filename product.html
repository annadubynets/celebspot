Britney Spears claims she was banned from drinking coffee during Las Vegas concerts

27 JAN 2022

Britney Spears has alleged that she was barred from drinking tea or coffee and forced to wear two pairs of tights during a lucrative residency she staged while her life was controlled by a conservatorship.

The pop star, 40, was released from the complex legal arrangement overseeing her personal and professional life in November. However, the fallout is still playing out in a Los Angeles court, where her lawyers are fighting her father, Jamie, over outstanding legal fees and claims the singer was placed under surveillance.
